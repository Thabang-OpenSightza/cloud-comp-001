The Problem We Are Solving

Understanding the African Context

🏢 For SMEs

Lack of access to large upfront capital (R30,000 - R150,000+) means they cannot afford custom software or AI. They are trapped using inefficient manual processes (WhatsApp, Excel) or expensive, generic foreign SaaS tools that don't fit local workflows.

🏛️ For Enterprises

Lengthy CapEx procurement cycles delay digital transformation by months. Furthermore, once an agency is paid upfront, they have no financial incentive to maintain or update the software long-term.

🤖 The AI Divide

African businesses know they need AI to stay competitive, but lack the in-house expertise to implement it safely and effectively.

💸 The "Sunk Cost" Trap

Businesses pay R50,000 for an app, but because they lack in-house tech teams to maintain it, the app breaks or becomes obsolete within a year.

Core Financial Mechanics

The "Zero-Risk" Rent-to-Buy Model

How It Works

1. Cloud Infrastructure

Client creates their own GCP account and attaches their credit card. Cost: R150-R500/month paid directly to Google. Hosted in africa-south1 (Johannesburg).

2. Care & Portal Retainer

R3,500/month flat fee covering Client Portal access, server monitoring, security patches, bug fixes, and up to 3 hours of minor feature tweaks.

3. Rent-to-Buy Amortization

Total buyout value: R48,000. R2,000/month credited toward buyout. R1,500 retained by OpenSightZA. Full IP transfer after 24 months.

Unit Economics (Per Client / Per Month)

Metric Amount Notes
Gross Retainer Collected R3,500 Paid by client via automated debit order
Less: IP Buydown Credit (R2,000) Non-cash credit reducing the R48k buyout balance
Net MRR (Gross Profit) R1,500 Pure recurring revenue for OpenSightZA
Less: OpenSightZA Cloud/Tool Costs (R100) Our share of GCP/AI API overhead per client
Net Profit per Client R1,400 / month R16,800 annual profit per client
At just 20 clients: OpenSightZA generates R28,000 in pure, predictable monthly profit, with zero server risk.

SWOT Analysis

Strategic Position Assessment

💪 Strengths

  • Frictionless Sales Cycle - Zero upfront CapEx removes biggest objection
  • High Client Retention ("Golden Handcuffs") - IP amortization disincentivizes churn
  • Zero Infrastructure Risk - Client-owned GCP billing protects us
  • Local Data Sovereignty - GCP Johannesburg region compliance edge

⚠️ Weaknesses

  • Front-Loaded Cash Flow - Heavy upfront hours before MRR compounds
  • Complex Legal Framework - Requires bulletproof SLAs
  • Onboarding Friction - Guiding non-technical SMEs through GCP setup

🌟 Opportunities

  • Government & NGO Grants - SETA digitalization grants favor OpEx models
  • AIAA Upsell Penetration - Increase ARPU by 40-60%
  • White-Labeling - Templatized architectures for multiple clients

🛑 Threats

  • Economic Downturns - SMEs may default on monthly retainers
  • AI Commoditization - Basic AIAA services may lose value
  • Cloud Provider Policy Changes - GCP IAM/billing structure changes
